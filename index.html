    Who is Jordan York?

    Jordan York: A Pittsburgh Media Personality

    Jordan York was voted 2 x Pittsburgh Best Pop Artist and has been featured on numerous radio, publications and television stations throughout his career.

    • York is best known for his love of all things Pittsburgh.
    • He is the Co-Founder of YinzerCrazy.com & Co-Host of the Sick Podcast Steelers Crazy.
    • He is also involved in the community, often volunteering at numerous events and non-profits in Pennsylvania.

    He has been seen/heard on: KDKA-CBS, Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, Pittsburgh Today Live, Pittsburgh Pirates, Pittsburgh Steelers, NFL, ESPN, Yahoo, The Source Magazine, Netflix & More!
